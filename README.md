## 🧠 About Me

I'm a **Product Designer** with 2 years of experience crafting intuitive, research-driven digital experiences. I bridge the gap between design thinking and modern frontend implementation — turning user insights into polished, accessible interfaces.

> I believe great design is invisible. It's the experience people don't notice because it just *works*.

- 🎨 I design with empathy — every pixel has a purpose
- 🚀 I've shipped end-to-end products from user research to live deployment
- 🤖 I'm currently exploring AI-powered UX and intelligent product design
- 📸 I share my learnings on [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/rewire.harsha/) & create content around design, growth, and mindset
- 📍 Always iterating — on products, ideas, and myself
